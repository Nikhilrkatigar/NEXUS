Assessment Rules & Regulations
Please read these carefully before you continue. Once you accept, you will enter the waiting room until the event head starts the assessment.

Before the exam starts

Stay on this page after logging in. The assessment will begin only when the event head starts it from the CMS.
Use only your assigned register number and password for this assessment.

During the exam

The assessment contains 20 questions with a 20-minute time limit.
Do not switch apps/tabs, open developer tools, copy, paste, or use restricted shortcuts.
On mobile, split-screen, popup view, or floating window modes are not allowed during the exam.
Do not attempt screenshots, screen recording, screen sharing, or any external aid during the exam.
Keep the browser active. The event head can stop the assessment for everyone from the CMS if needed.

Disqualification policy

Violations are monitored. More than 2 violations will automatically disqualify your attempt.
If you break the rules, your attempt may be submitted automatically and marked as disqualified.
